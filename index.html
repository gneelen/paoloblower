About

Our Story

Founded in 1982, Paolo Blower International Textile Workshop quickly established itself as an industry pioneer that offered Italian quality fabrics to the American contemporary and newly-born bridge markets.

Over three decades our firm has expanded its selection to include fabrics from many other countries, thereby adjusting to market changes, economic downturns, and most current client needs. One thing is certain: we have never lost sight of our original vision to bring innovation, quality, undisputed style, and superior customer service to both our customers and suppliers.

Today, our team of young dynamic professionals and seasoned management still operates from our historical offices in midtown Manhattan and Prato, Italy, where we constantly monitor new sources, detect latest fashion trends and maintain the global perspective that keeps Paolo Blower a most reputable and inspiring leader in the marketplace.
